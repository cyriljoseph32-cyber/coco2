Practical Guide

Everything You Need to Know

Essential facts for a smooth, safe and enjoyable stay on Koh Samui.

💰
Currency & Money
Thai Baht (THB). 1 EUR ≈ 37–38 THB. ATMs everywhere (220 THB fee per withdrawal). Get larger amounts at once. Most tourist spots accept cards.
🚗
Getting Around
Songthaew (shared truck) 50–150 THB. Taxis 200–500 THB (negotiate). Grab app saves 20–30%. Motorbike 200–300 THB/day — drive carefully, high accident rate.
🌤️
Best Time to Visit
Dec–April: dry season, clear blue skies, best for diving. May–Sept: warm, some rain but usually sunny. Oct–Nov: monsoon season, avoid if possible.
📱
SIM & Connectivity
Buy AIS SIM at the airport (best coverage). 299 THB/30 days unlimited data. Download Grab before you arrive — you'll use it every day.
🛕
Temple Etiquette
Cover shoulders and knees. Remove shoes before entering. Don't point feet at Buddha images. A sarong or scarf can be bought near temples (~100 THB).
🏥
Health & Safety
Bangkok Hospital Samui (most reliable). Emergency: 1669. Tourist police (English): 1155. Travel insurance strongly recommended for diving and water activities.
🚢
Ferries & Islands
Koh Phangan: 30 min, ~400 THB from Bangrak Pier. Koh Tao: 2 hrs, ~750 THB. Surat Thani mainland: 2.5 hrs, ~300 THB from Nathon Pier.
🌊
Diving Safety
Only dive with PADI 5-Star centres. Never dive after alcohol. Reef-safe biodegradable sunscreen only. Check weather before sea trips in Oct–Nov.
