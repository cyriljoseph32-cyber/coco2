The Best of Koh Samui

Handpicked experiences that make Koh Samui unforgettable — from underwater wonders to hilltop sunsets.

🤿
Sail Rock Diving
One of Southeast Asia's top dive sites — a towering pinnacle with whale shark sightings, giant groupers and crystal-clear visibility year-round.
From 2,500 THB
🏝️
Ang Thong Marine Park
80 islands, emerald lagoons and jungle trails. Kayak into hidden caves, snorkel pristine reefs — a full day adventure 35km northwest of Samui.
From 1,800 THB
🌅
Secret Buddha Garden
A surreal hilltop garden of statues built by a local farmer in the 1970s. Jungle trails, misty views and total tranquility — Samui's most unique cultural site.
Free–80 THB
