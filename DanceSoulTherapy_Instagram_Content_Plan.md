## 🎠 POST 2 — Carousel: "Mother–daughter session recap"

**Format:** Carousel, 5–6 slides (real or staged mother–daughter duo).
**Angle:** Talk about the *bond*, not the technique — what opens up between a mother and daughter when they move together instead of talk.

**Slide breakdown**
- **Slide 1** — Warm-up together, side by side. Text: *"What happens when a mother and daughter move — instead of talk."*
- **Slide 2** — A laughing moment mid-movement (candid, not posed).
- **Slide 3** — Hands connecting / mirroring each other's movement.
- **Slide 4** — A still, close-embrace moment. Let it breathe.
- **Slide 5 — Quote slide:** *"Movement is the language before words."*

## 📖 POST 3 — Story series: "What's blocking you from moving freely?"

**Format:** Instagram Stories, 3–4 frames. Fills the gap between bigger posts and keeps the DM inbox warm.
**Angle:** Low-effort interaction → gentle emotional truth → soft invitation.

**Frame breakdown**
- **Frame 1 — Poll:** *"Do you dance when no one's watching?"* — options **Yes / Never**.
- **Frame 2 — Text over movement clip:** *"Most of us stop moving freely the moment we think someone's watching."*
- **Frame 3 — Text/quote:** *"Your body remembers how to release — even when your mind forgot."*
- **Frame 4 — Soft CTA:** *"Private and duo sessions on Samui 🤍"* + link sticker / *"DM 'FEEL' to book."*

**Copy notes**
> Keep it conversational and unpolished — Stories should feel like a voice note, not an ad.
> Reply to every poll vote with a short DM; that's where sessions actually get booked.

**Hashtags (1–2 max on Stories):** #dancesoultherapy #kohsamui

**CTA:** Reply to the poll or DM **"FEEL"**; link sticker to book.

## ✅ Shoot checklist (one session captures all three)
- [ ] Film vertical 9:16; soft natural light — early morning on the beach or a calm studio corner.
- [ ] Barefoot, natural/flowing clothing; avoid anything that reads as a "performance" costume.
- [ ] Capture slow, feeling-led movement — sway, hands on heart, exhale/release — not choreography.
- [ ] For the carousel: get candid mother–daughter moments (laughing, embrace), not posed portraits.
- [ ] Get consent from anyone recognizable on camera, especially the mother–daughter duo.
- [ ] Keep on-screen text minimal and gentle; let the movement carry the feeling.
- [ ] Same CTA voice everywhere: soft, invitational — **DM "FEEL"**, never pushy.

> Posting rhythm: **Reel (Post 1)** to introduce the "what is this" → **Carousel (Post 2)** for depth and the duo offer → run the **Story series (Post 3)** in between to keep DMs warm. Space the two main posts ~4–5 days apart.
